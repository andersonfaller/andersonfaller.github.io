// Interests

AI & Physics

My research lies at the intersection of rigorous physical modeling and data-driven approaches. I apply Machine Learning to solve complex inverse problems in fluid dynamics and multiphase flow, bridging the gap between experimental data and theoretical models.

Programming

I view code as a medium for structure and logic. From architecting robust full-stack web solutions to developing scientific computing pipelines in Python, I focus on writing clean, maintainable software that solves complex problems efficiently.

Language Models

I am highly interested in the practical applications of Large Language Models (LLMs) within engineering and scientific domains. My focus is on leveraging these models to optimize technical workflows and assist in complex problem-solving. Previously, I also explored AI safety and alignment through my work on DetoxLM.

Chess

An amateur player with a deep appreciation for the game's complexity. My interest extends beyond the board into chess programming and engine development. Challenge me on Chess.com or Lichess.

Abstract Strategy Games

Beyond chess, I explore the mechanics of games like Tak and Lines of Action (LoA). I enjoy analyzing their unique emergent complexity. Check out my simple LoA engine implementation here.

Music & Instruments

Music provides a great counterbalance to technical work. My musical taste leans towards heavy metal, progressive rock, and indie rock. I enjoy the discipline of learning instruments and the creative energy of playing. You can listen to some of my casual musical experiments on SoundCloud.

Pop Cosmology & Astrophysics

Fascinated by the vastness of the universe, black holes, and the origins of spacetime. I enjoy reading accessible literature on the subject to grasp the macro-scale mechanics of our reality and our place within it.

Pop Quantum Physics

Intrigued by the counter-intuitive nature of the quantum realm. I enjoy exploring concepts like entanglement and superposition through popular science, balancing my deterministic engineering background with the probabilistic wonders of nature.
